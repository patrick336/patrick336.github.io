Welcome to TDK Trade Frames Ltd...

With combined experience of 40 years in the window, conservatory and glass industry TDK Trade Frames can supply high quality products backed by the highest levels of customer service. Based in Poole but servicing a wide area from Weymouth, Salisbury and Southampton

doors

Composite doors offer a tough, practical and long-lasting alternative to traditional wood. From classic designs to more modern aesthetics, composite doors are a contemporary solution for all interior and exterior door needs.

Sometimes you need something a little special for a job that doesn’t conform to the usual specifications. So at Indigo Products we offer a personalised bespoke design and planning service. It’s all part of our commitment to give you the best possible customer experience from start to finish.

why tdk doors?

  1. Greater thermal efficiency, keeping the warmth in and energy bills down
  2. Stronger, more secure doors with multi-lock security for total peace of mind
  3. Easy care – no need to paint so no additional use of harmful chemicals
  4. Eco-friendly – because composite doors use man-made materials there is less impact on the world’s fragile forest ecosystems, and doors are also recyclable to keep your carbon footprint down
  5. No compromise on aesthetics
